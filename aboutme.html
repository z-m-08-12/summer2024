About Me


Welcome to my page! My name is Zacharie. I commonly use the alias “limegreen” online. I am an artist who dabbles in everything from music production to game development to now web design! Primarily, though, my focus is on character art and design. Until I have more examples of my other works, this site will contain mostly my sketches and occasional school assignments or other traditional work sprinkled in. Thank you for visiting and taking the time to check out my art. I hope to develop this site further in the future.
